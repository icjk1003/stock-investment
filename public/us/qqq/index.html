Is QQQ a dividend ETF?

Usually no. It‚Äôs more of a growth/price-return ETF. Dividends exist, but total return is the main story.

How is it different from SPY (or VOO)?

SPY/VOO tracks S&P 500 with broader diversification. QQQ is more tech/growth tilted and can be more volatile.

What do backtest/future sims assume?

Backtest uses historical price/dividend where available with recurring buys. Future sim uses your assumptions (return/tax/contribution).

Does my account type matter in the US?

Yes. Taxable accounts differ from retirement accounts (e.g., IRA/401k) in tax treatment and compounding behavior.
