üìå User Guide for SCHD Calculator

This simulator is designed to forecast the long-term performance of SCHD (Schwab U.S. Dividend Equity ETF). It provides an integrated view of capital appreciation and the compound effects of dividend reinvestment (DRIP).

üìä Key Calculation Logic

  • Compound Capital Gain: Applied monthly based on the annual price growth rate.
  • Net Dividend Reinvestment: Net dividends after tax (15%) are reinvested into the principal monthly.
  • Dividend Growth: Reflects SCHD's characteristic of increasing payouts annually.
