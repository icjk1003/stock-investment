Why show CAD and USD?

SCHD is quoted in USD, but many Canadian investors evaluate wealth in CAD. Even if SCHD performs the same in USD, USD/CAD movements can materially change your CAD result.

Is SCHD a ‚Äúhigh yield‚Äù ETF?

SCHD is usually positioned more as a dividend growth ETF than a pure high-yield product. The goal is typically quality + rising dividends, not maximizing yield at all costs.

What do backtest/future sims assume?

Backtest uses historical prices (and dividends where available) with recurring buys. Future sim uses your assumptions (return, contributions, taxes, FX) to project outcomes.
